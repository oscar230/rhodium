Gathered from Houben-Weyl by GC_MS

Introduction

What follows is a compilation of omega-nitrostyrene syntheses taken from Houben-Weyl. There are 4 sections:

  • Benzaldehydes with Nitromethane
  • Benzaldehydes with Nitroethane
  • Benzaldehydes with Nitropropane
  • Benzaldehydes with Nitrobutane

The "Parts" subdivision of the section agree with different pages from Houben-Weyl.
Hence, many references are mentioned several times.
